# Literature Review Notes

## Photocurrent Induced by a Bicircular Light Drive in Centrosymmetric System

> Here we show that an application of a BCL to centrosymmetric systems allows a photocurrent generation through introduction of an effective _polarity_ to the system
> 
> BCL driving can introduce polarity in centrosymmetric systems and induce electric polarization due to **inversion symmetry** breaking originating from incompatible rotation symmetry of the BCL

BCL with C3_v symmetry breaks the **time-reversal symmetry and the inversion symmetry**. The broken of symmetry induces a effective polarity and generates a photoncurrent.
